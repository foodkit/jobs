Job Description

We're looking for an experienced frontend developer to join the Foodkit team. You'll use the latest versions of Angular to build performant and responsive user interfaces for restaurant owners/operators and their customers.

You'll be helping us to build out new features on a number of codebases, including consumer facing websites, back-of-house content management systems and even hybrid mobile applications. Our customers use our platform to operate their business online so it's crucial we provide rock-solid software that works across a broad range of devices and operating systems.

As a remote worker, you can pick where and when you work, as long as you stay productive and we have some overlapping hours to do standups.
